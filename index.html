How it works?

Register on our platform to start meeting people worldwide. Create your profile, specify your interests, and begin connecting with like-minded individuals.

Step 1: You quiz yourself.

The journey starts with a series of fun but deep psychographic quizzes. We ask everything from your taste in music to how annoyed you get by spelling and grammer grammar mistakes.

Step 2: we do the matching.

Our algorithm incorporates hundreds of factors in the matching process, making your groups super likely to get along. You can also check out each group’s overall compatibility, from mutual quirks to shared life goals.

Step 3: Chat & hang out.

As you join groups, you can safely vibe in the group-chat before deciding to meet up. We encourage hanging out in the real world* because that’s how you really get to know someone.
